SECA Zones

Sulphur Emission Control Areas require fuel with maximum 0.1% sulphur content. These areas have the strictest regulations.

  • North Sea SECA
  • Baltic Sea SECA
  • All US ECA regions
  • US Caribbean ECA

ECA Zones

Emission Control Areas with 0.5% sulphur limit. Less strict than SECA but still require special fuel.

  • Mediterranean ECA
  • Other regional ECAs
0.5%

Global Cap

IMO 2020 Global Sulphur Cap requires 0.5% sulphur fuel worldwide, outside of ECAs. This is the baseline standard.

  • All international waters
  • Areas not designated as ECA
  • Baseline from Jan 1, 2020
